The app is a free, accountless 24/7 lo-fi radio client.

Review steps:
1. Launch the app while connected to the internet.
2. Wait for the current track metadata to appear.
3. Press the center/OK button on the Siri Remote to start playback.
4. The player seeks to the shared server clock so playback matches https://app.lofiever.dev.
5. Press the center/OK button again to pause.

No login, purchase, subscription, microphone, camera, or location permission is required. Privacy and support information is available from the footer inside the app.

Production API: https://app.lofiever.dev/api/stream
Support: https://app.lofiever.dev/pt/support
